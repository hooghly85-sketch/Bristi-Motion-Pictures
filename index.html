About Us

BRISTI MOTION PICTURES is a creative film production house based in India, dedicated to cinematic storytelling, short films, music videos, web series and digital content.

We focus on emotional storytelling, strong visuals and professional editing to create impactful cinematic experiences.

Founded by Joy Das with co-producer Sukumar Das, our mission is to grow as an independent production house delivering high-quality visual stories.
